Imagine you are in a Art gallery and you want details of a painting in an immersive way. What do you do? Well, now you can just open your the app and recieve relevant information such as the name of the art, the artist and the time duration between which it was created.
The current draft of the project is very basic and is made to detect only:
1 .Vincent van Gogh, The Starry Night
2. Georges Seurat, A Sunday Afternoon on the Island of La Grande Jatte

*Use an image from web of the above given Artworks to use the app*
It has only image trained for the above 2 arts
